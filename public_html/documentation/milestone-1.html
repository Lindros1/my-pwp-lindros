Purpose

The purpose of my personal website project is to display my professional portfolio to potential employers. I plan on developing this site to accomplish my goal of obtaining employment after I am finished with the bootcamp.

Audience

This site will be designed for potential employers, developers, customers and/or clients in mind when they view my site. Content on the site will be displayed in a unique way yet the information will still be simply conveyed to the audience viewing the site.

Goal

My major goal with this is to assist with obtaining a full time role within a company. A minor goal would be to obtain a part-time role.

Persona

Sharon Dawson Age: 49

Profession

Middle manager at a company that is in need of a junior web developer. Mid-level manager for the tech company Row, a well established company looking to hire a full-time junior web developer. They have just relocated to Amarillo and are seeking local talent. Their product is an online augmented reality education tool marketed to be a disruption in the education industry.

Technology

2021 Mac desktop in office. 2020 MacBook Pro laptops for remote work. 2019 iPhone 10 with an unlimited plan.

Attitudes and Behaviors

Has a normal manageable workload. Sharon spends approximately 5 minutes scrolling through a candidates' portfolio site, and about a minute reviewing a resume. Sharon loves to use social media along with LinkedIn to seek potential candidates through different tech community's and networking events.

Frustrations and Needs

Sharon is looking to hire someone with a year or less experience so that they can use the newer talent to stay longer with the company. The job pays an entry-level salary, to start. Sharon is also looking for someone who has the right personality that will fit in with the current company culture. Sharon has a short deadline and is looking for a qualified individual with a limited talent pool to choose from.

Goals

Sharon's main goal is to hire an individual that is qualified and will stay with the company long term. Sharon is constantly speeding up here screening process to find an individual who is a good fit for the company and weather or not they are worth a phone call.

User Story

Coming from a user, I need to determine quickly and efficiently if an individual can fulfill our Junior Web Developer position to our standards.
